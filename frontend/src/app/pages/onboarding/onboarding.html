Getting Started

Welcome to the Coffee House team. This page gives new employees a simple overview of what to do on their first day.

What to bring

  • Government-issued ID
  • Work authorization documents
  • Notebook and pen

First day checklist

  • Meet the store manager
  • Review store policies and safety rules
  • Get uniform and workstation tour
  • Learn POS system basics

Training areas

  • Customer service
  • Drink preparation
  • Cash handling
  • Cleaning and closing duties
